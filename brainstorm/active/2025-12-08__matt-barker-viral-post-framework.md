## Core Framework

Matt's key insight on what makes posts go viral:

1. **Broadly understandable topics** - money, relationships, morning routines, AI
2. **Simple idea and packaging** - easy for anyone to read
3. **The post itself matters** - not timing, not engagement hacks

> "ANYONE can understand these posts. You don't need to know anything about the topic at hand."

## Key Takeaways for Our Framework

### What Makes Matt's Posts Viral:

1. **Specificity** - Real numbers, real names, real tools
2. **Relatability** - Problems everyone has (money, productivity, authenticity)
3. **Structure** - Lists, timelines, before/after
4. **Humor** - Self-deprecating, not try-hard
5. **Contrarian angle** - Against hustle culture, against fake LinkedIn behavior
6. **Easy to scan** - Short lines, bullet points, clear sections

### Post Types That Work:

| Type | Example | Engagement |
|------|---------|------------|
| Anti-hustle manifesto | Post 5 | 1,010 |
| Revenue transparency | Post 4 | 783 |
| Relatable disaster | Post 2 | 768 |
| Life transformation | Post 6 | 756 |
| Revenue breakdown | Post 1 | 628 |
| Contrarian critique | Post 3 | 447 |

### Patterns to Adopt:

1. **Transparency posts** - Share real numbers, real tools, real process
2. **Anti-perfection** - Show the messy reality, not polished version
3. **Timeline transformations** - Before/after with specific milestones
4. **Contrarian takes** - Call out what everyone sees but doesn't say
5. **Tool stacks** - Actionable lists people can use

### Hooks That Work:

- "I'm [age]. [Situation]. [Unexpected detail]."
- "My [goal]: [specific number]. I'm at [progress]."
- "[Time period] ago: [list of old state]"
- "I'm a simple guy. I don't need to:"
- "[Person] did [thing]. It was [strong opinion]."


## Application Notes

For our content creation framework:
- These patterns work for **personal brand** content
- Translate to tech/engineering audience by swapping topics
- Key: specificity + relatability + easy to scan
- Avoid: generic advice, abstract concepts, no numbers
