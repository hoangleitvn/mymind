INNOMIZE AI Adoption Workshop 05
Step 1

Start with the Workflow

Where does input come from?

Customer request? Internal trigger? Scheduled event?

What decisions get made?

Explicit approvals and implicit judgments

What does "done" look like?

Format, quality level, required information

Where does validation happen?

Checkpoints where errors get caught

Who owns the final call?

Accountability for the outcome

If you cannot draw the workflow, you cannot automate it.
