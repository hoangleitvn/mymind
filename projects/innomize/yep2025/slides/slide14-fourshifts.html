The 4 Mental Shifts

Career growth is not about titles. It is about what you deliver.

JUNIOR

Deliver a Task

Learn fast
Execute reliably
Grow steadily

MIDDLE

Deliver a Project

Understand scope
Own delivery
Coordinate with team

SENIOR / LEAD

Deliver Standards

Set best practices
Mentor others
Improve process

MANAGEMENT

Deliver a Team

Enable people
Align priorities
Drive through others

The title follows the mindset, not the other way around.
