Mindset Shift

  • From "waiting for task" to "what should I learn next?"
  • From "I don't know what I don't know" to "I will find out"
  • From blaming (company, others) to owning your growth

  • Understand the system before you're asked
  • Raise issues before they become problems
  • Ask for help. Do not wait to be rescued
  • Have a plan, not just wishes
