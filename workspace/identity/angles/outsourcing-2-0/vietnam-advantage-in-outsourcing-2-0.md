# Angle: Vietnam Advantage in Outsourcing 2.0

**Theme:** Outsourcing 2.0

## Position

Vietnam advantage realized through Outsourcing 2.0 model: 50-60% cost savings + quality + accountability. Traditional outsourcing model failed with Vietnam same as everywhere else. Embedded ownership model + Vietnam talent = cost efficiency without traditional problems (communication gaps, quality issues, lack of accountability).

## Sharp Pillars

- Vietnam: strong technical talent, English proficiency, 50-60% cost advantage
- Model matters more than location (wrong model fails everywhere)
