What Actually Happens

What People Expect

  • AI makes work 10x faster
  • Therefore: work 10x less
  • Same output, less effort

What Actually Happens

  • AI makes work 10x faster
  • We tackle 10x more complex problems
  • Workload stays same, capability expands

The Right Question

Don't ask "How much time does AI save?" - Ask "What can we build now that we couldn't before?"
