The Productivity Reality

"AI changed the work, not the workload."

What People Expect

  • AI makes work 10x faster
  • Therefore: work 10x less
  • Same output, less effort

What Actually Happens

  • AI makes work 10x faster
  • We tackle 10x more complex problems
  • Workload stays same, capability expands

The Right Question

The 4 Concerns We Address

1. Workflow & Skills

If the team doesn't understand what "done" looks like, AI creates mess and bugs. We need workflow before tools.

2. Validation

Without standards and best practices, how do we validate and review AI-assisted work? We need review workflows.

3. Data Privacy

Client project data, internal HR and Finance data — clear boundaries are needed before scaling AI usage.

4. Cost

Tool purchasing decisions should follow skill development. Evaluate ROI before buying.
