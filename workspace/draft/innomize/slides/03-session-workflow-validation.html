Why Workflow Matters

Without Workflow

  • AI generates output
  • You accept it (looks fine)
  • Errors accumulate
  • Problems discovered later
  • Expensive to fix

With Workflow

  • AI generates output
  • Validation at defined points
  • Errors caught early
  • Quality maintained
  • Cheap to fix
The goal: Trust the process, not the output.

Validation Gates

Where should validation happen?

5
INNOMIZE

Always Validate

  • Facts and numbers — AI hallucinates
  • Policy/legal references — Especially Vietnam-specific
  • Customer-facing content — Brand and reputation
  • Financial calculations — Errors compound
  • Anything hard to reverse — Prevention is cheaper

Rule of Thumb

If it's going outside your team, validate it.

Validation Methods

Checklist

3-5 must-have criteria

  • Quick to run through
  • Consistent across team
  • Catches common errors

Peer Review

Second pair of eyes

  • Catches blind spots
  • Creates feedback loops
  • Shares knowledge

Source Verification

Check against official docs

  • Policy references
  • Legal requirements
  • Current data

Test Case

Does it work in practice?

  • Run a sample
  • Try edge cases
  • Verify output format

Validation-Debt

Unverified AI output accumulating in your work

8
INNOMIZE

Signs of Validation-Debt

  • "I think AI wrote that, but I'm not sure"
  • Errors discovered weeks later
  • Can't explain why something is the way it is
  • Fear of changing AI-generated content
  • Growing pile of content nobody trusts

The Problem

Validation-debt accumulates silently. You don't notice until something breaks.

9
INNOMIZE

Preventing Validation-Debt

1

Validate Before Moving

Check output before proceeding to next step. Don't batch validation at the end.

2

Document What You Verified

Brief note: "Checked against policy doc X on [date]"

3

Own the Output

Your name, your responsibility. AI is a tool, not an author.

"I used AI to help" is fine. "AI wrote it" is not accountability.

INNOMIZE

Key Takeaways

1. Design the workflow before using AI

Know the steps, validation points, and "done" criteria.

2. Add validation gates at key points

Facts, policy, customer-facing content, financials.

3. Validate before moving, not at the end

Catch errors early when they're cheap to fix.

4. You own the output, not AI

Your name, your responsibility.
