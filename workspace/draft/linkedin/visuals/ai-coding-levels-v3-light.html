0

1
"AI assists my coding"

2
"AI writes code I review"

MOST STUCK HERE
3
"AI follows my instructions"

4
"AI solves my problem"
You specify WHAT and WHY. AI figures out HOW.
5
"AI reasons about the system"
You give context and constraints. AI makes decisions. You validate.
