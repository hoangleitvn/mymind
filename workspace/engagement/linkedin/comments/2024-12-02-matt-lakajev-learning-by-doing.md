## Original Post

I'm 34, from Sydney, running my online business, 7 Figure Creators. My wife, who runs a global 8 figure business, gave me the best advice when I started "You've got to get in there and just do everything yourself." I agree with her completely. Our business is 100% bootstrapped from the $180,000 I put into it. Here's exactly what I sell, how I sell it, and the tools I use.

STREET CRED: I've coached 1,750 small business owners who together generate $172 million a year on LinkedIn. We have more than 1,200 testimonials.

AUDIENCE: 100,000 LinkedIn followers, 47,653 email subscribers

WHAT IS SELL: Our 7 Figure Creators program for $897 for 3 months, then $299 a month after that.

WHAT I ACTUALLY SELL: I teach adults how to become confident and talk to humans on LinkedIn to grow their business

MARKETING: I send 3 emails a week. I do 2x live workshops every 3 weeks. I post on LinkedIn 2x day, 7 days a week. My last workshop went for 12 hours and 47 minutes. Ohâ€¦our whole team are female salespeople and community managers, and they crush.

HOW I CLOSE: 1) You buy from a Notion doc OR 2) You buy from a Google Doc on a workshop live. You can never book a sales call with me. The only fireable offence in our business is if someone books a time on my calendar.

TOOLS: GrowthMode, the CRM we built for LinkedIn; LinkNinja, the software we built for LinkedIn; Slack, Loom, Zoom. Also Claude, Grok, ChatGPT and Gemini to keep me honest so I don't blow up my own ego.

THE TRUTH: In the first 8 mths, I burned $180k of my own money. 0 idea what I was doing. And I won't lie. I'm still learning daily. It we signed 150 new clients last month and they're getting insane results, so we're going 10x on the 1 price in 2025.

After 3 years of doing this, the main thing I've learned is that you don't learn anything just by reading or watching videos. You learn by doing things. That's it lol.
