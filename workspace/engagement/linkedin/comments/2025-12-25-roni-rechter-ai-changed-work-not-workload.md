## Original Post

AI Tools Changed The Work, Not The Workload AI generates code and docs way faster than writing manually. Massive time savings on creation. But now we spend time on different stuff: validating outputs, catching edge cases, making sure generated code actually works in production. And here's what people missâ€”you still need to design the right architecture. AI pumps out components fast, but someone needs to figure out how they fit together, what patterns make sense, where the boundaries are. Still faster overall. But the work went from "writing everything" to "architecting and reviewing what AI generates, fixing the 10-20% that's off." Different skill set. Different workflow. I see that with the same team, we build systems that would've been impossible before. The complexity we can handle went up significantly. Its worth it. Shipping faster, handling more complexity. Just not the "automate everything" story people sell. #AI #SoftwareEngineering #ProductionAI
