## Original Post

Most people stay stuck because they overthink.

These 7 visuals will change how you take action:

1) Fear spikes right before you begin
- Notice the tightness that shows up before you act.
- Step toward it. It signals you're expanding.

2) Admit you're afraid and move forward anyway
- Take one tiny action today.
- Fear loses its power once you're in motion.

3) You're capable of far more than you think
- Write down three strengths you've been overlooking.
- Put one of them to use this week.

4) Growth feels slow until it suddenly accelerates
- Commit to one habit for the next 30 days.
- Breakthroughs arrive after consistency.

5) Think beyond the immediate moment
- Every decision either benefits or limits your future self.
- Choose with your three-year future in mind.

6) Most of our suffering comes from imagined scenarios
- Notice one unhelpful thought and reframe it.
- List three things you appreciate right now.

7) Pick the route that stretches you
- The more challenging option is usually the better one.
- Your future self will be glad you chose it.

Pick one of the seven and start today.

Number 2 is my favorite.

Which one resonates most with you?
