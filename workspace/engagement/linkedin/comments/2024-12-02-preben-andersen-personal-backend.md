## Original Post

What will separate the top performers in the future?

AI companies today are competing on context and so should you.

A lot seem to point towards top performers having their own personal backend.

A toolkit they bring with them wherever they go.

We are already seeing a lightweight version of this today.

To get the most out of my agents (like Cursor, Claude and Comet) I maintain and update context, rules and prompts that make me more effective.

This will expand to be about build agents that will automate bigger parts of our job in the background.

Then we will need to manage a suite of agents.

This stack becomes an extension of you and it gets sharper on every update/iteration.

Capabilities and efficiencies compound.

Maybe this personal backend is what future employees are evaluated on?

Request for startups: Make it easy for employees to manage their personal backend.
