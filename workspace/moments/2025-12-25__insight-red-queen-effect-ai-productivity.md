# Insight: The Red Queen Effect of AI Productivity

## The Moment

Comment about Red Queen Effect on AI productivity got noticed and liked. Other commenters referenced related concepts:
- Jevon's Paradox
- Red Queen's race
- Arms race dynamics
- Parkinson's Law
- Laws of thermodynamics (metaphorically)
- Systems far from equilibrium

Different disciplines, similar dynamics: **relative goals can recede as quickly as we pursue them.**

## The Core Insight

Everyone asks: "How much time does AI save?"

Wrong question.

Ask: "What can we build now that we couldn't before?"

That's the Red Queen insight: you don't get time back. You get capability. And capability compounds.
