## Original Post

Dear junior developers, stop racing to write code. (you'll thank me later)

I see it all the time: new devs think speed equals skill. They jump straight into coding and hope it works.

Here is the hard truth:
> writing code quickly doesn't make you better; it often makes you worse.

The secret is simple:
> understand the problem first.

Treat coding like the final step in a decision-making process, not the starting point.
