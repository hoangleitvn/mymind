Same concept, new face and often 5-10x the total views. UGC is not about being original every time. It's about finding what works and running it back. 5) Study trends like it's your job Half of our workflow is just watching what's trending. We track popular formats, viral hooks, and what's working for others. First we copy it, then we adapt it to our style. Guessing wastes time, start with what's proven. 6) Small creators > big influencers We work with creators who have fewer than 5K followers. They're faster, easier to work with, and much more motivated. 7) Pay based on results We keep it simple: a small base fee plus a bonus if the video performs. If the video works, everyone wins - if not, no big loss. 8) Track revenue, not likes Likes and views are nice, but they don't pay the bills.
