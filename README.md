# mymind

My mind, my brain - build, grow, learn, share on top of Mizemind.

## What this is

Personal workspace for thinking, creating, and sharing. Powered by [Mizemind](https://github.com/mizemind) Claude Code plugins.

## Structure

```
foundation/       Identity, voice, themes, personas
content/          Published and draft content by platform
engagement/       LinkedIn engagement tracking and relationships
brainstorm/       Ideas, research, and exploration
moments/          Captured insights and content seeds
inbox/            Unprocessed notes and research
archive/          Historical content and references
```

## Powered by

- **Claude Code** - AI-assisted content creation
- **Mizemind plugins** - LinkedIn engagement, content creation, identity management
