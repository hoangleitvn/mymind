In today's world, you don't need a separate translation app anymore. You can just use ChatGPT to bridge the language gap. Whether you’re speaking in Vietnamese, English, or any other language, here’s how you can turn on translator mode with Voice:

Use this prompt:

- In English: “Act as a translator between Vietnamese and English. When I speak Vietnamese, translate it into English. When the other person speaks English, translate it back into Vietnamese. Wait for each of us to finish before translating.

- In Vietnamese: “Hãy đóng vai một người phiên dịch giữa tiếng Việt và tiếng Anh. Khi tôi nói tiếng Việt, hãy dịch sang tiếng Anh. Khi người kia nói tiếng Anh, hãy dịch lại sang tiếng Việt. Hãy chờ mỗi bên nói xong rồi mới dịch.”

With this simple prompt, we will able to travel and communicate easily without any hassle. Whereable you are, you can use your native language to speak with the others speaking different languages.

Give it a try!
