Your brand isn't built through consistency of posting.
It's built through consistency of values.

People don't remember what you posted.
They remember how you made them think.

1. Define your expertise (what you actually know deeply)
2. Identify your audience's problems (not what you want to talk about)
3. Share solutions that create value (teach, don't preach)
4. Engage authentically (respond, don't broadcast)
5. Measure by impact, not vanity metrics

The strongest personal brands aren't built on perfection.

They're built on authenticity, generosity, and consistent value.
