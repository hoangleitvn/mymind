Serverless vs Containers

The 2025 Decision Tree

Lambda Standard

Pay-per-invocation

Choose when
  • Traffic is spiky or unpredictable
  • Can't commit to long-term pricing
  • Cold starts don't matter
  • Event-driven workloads

Lambda Managed

Serverless DX + container pricing

Choose when
  • Traffic is steady & predictable
  • Want Savings Plans (72% off)
  • Cold starts hurt UX
  • High-volume APIs

Containers

ECS / EKS

Choose when
  • Need full control over runtime
  • Complex dependencies
  • Custom runtimes required
  • Multi-cloud portability

The best architectures aren't pure. They're pragmatic.

Ask "which fits this workload" not "which is better"
