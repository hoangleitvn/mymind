AWS COST OPTIMIZATION

6 Strategies That Actually Work

Ranked by impact and ease of implementation

1

Right-sizing

20-40% savings

Match instance sizes to actual workload. m5.xlarge → m5.large = $140/month saved per instance.

2

Savings Plans

30-72% discount

Commit to usage for discounts. More flexible than Reserved Instances. Start at 50% baseline.

3

Auto-scaling

20-60% savings

Scale down when demand drops. Running 24/7 at peak = 60% waste during off-peak.

4

Spot Instances

60-90% savings

Spare AWS capacity at steep discounts. Best for batch jobs, CI/CD, dev environments.

5

Storage Tiering

40-80% savings

S3 Standard vs Deep Archive = 23x price difference. Set lifecycle policies once.

6

Serverless

varies

Cuts 50-80% for sporadic workloads. Can double costs for steady high-volume.

Right-sizing + Savings Plans Low effort, low risk
