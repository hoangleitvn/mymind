10 Questions Before
Building Anything

Most problems aren't technical. They're clarity problems.

Should I Build At All?
1
What problem am I solving?
Solutions without problems are expensive
2
Who is this for?
"Everyone" means no one
3
What if I don't build this?
Sometimes nothing bad happens
Build From Scratch?
4
What already exists?
Best code is code you don't write
5
Why did the reference choose this?
Copying outputs without inputs fails
What's The Right Scope?
6
What's the simplest version?
Start minimum, expand with evidence
How Do I Validate?
7
How will I know it works?
No criteria = no improvement
8
What can break?
Think failure before it finds you
What Happens After I Ship?
9
Who will maintain this?
Building is easy. Owning is hard.
10
How will others understand this?
You won't be here forever.
