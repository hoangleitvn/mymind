# NNOMIZERS kick off 2025 with Tech Day: Understanding DevSecOps

To start the new year with innovation and knowledge sharing, INNOMIZERS successfully hosted the first Tech Day of 2025 with the theme **"Understanding DevSecOps"**. This session aimed to emphasize the importance of security in software development and provide valuable insights, best practices, and tools to support a secure development lifecycle.

 
 ![](https://res.cloudinary.com/innomizetech/image/upload/v1740468257/production/DSC_08074_Large_9b135dd4a8.jpg) 

 
 ![](https://res.cloudinary.com/innomizetech/image/upload/v1740468258/production/DSC_08169_Large_184c66661f.jpg) 

During the event, our experts highlighted the core principles of DevSecOps, the benefits of integrating security early in development, and how to leverage powerful tools like SonarQube and Snyk to enhance code security. Attendees had the opportunity to engage in discussions, share experiences, and gain practical knowledge on improving software security.
