Skills That Rise in Value

McKinsey analysis of 800+ occupations

Increasing Demand

  • Problem-solving
  • Communication and negotiation
  • Leadership and collaboration
  • Coaching and empathy
  • AI fluency (fastest-growing)

Exposed to Automation

  • Routine digital tasks
  • Information processing
  • Rules-based work
  • Data entry and formatting

Key Finding

72% of skills employers seek are used in BOTH automatable and non-automatable work. The future is skill partnership, not replacement.
