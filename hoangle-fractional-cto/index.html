About Hoang

I started as a software engineer and quickly learned the hard truths of "work about work" in engineering. Instead of waiting for permission, I led client conversations, solved problems directly, and saw how fragile projects became when ownership was missing.

In 2018, I founded INNOMIZE with one goal: build with accountability, not hourly excuses. Today, we've shipped 50+ products with a team of 40+ senior engineers, helping founders go from zero to live users.

When you work with me, you get the strategic thinking of a fractional CTO and, when needed, the execution capacity of INNOMIZE. It's embedded ownership, not traditional outsourcing.
