# Custom Hooks

Personal hook formulas for sales-focused content. These hooks work best for client acquisition, case studies, and offer-related posts.

---

## Client-Focused Hooks

### client-problem-to-goal

**Formula:** How I tackled [ICP problem] to [ICP goal].
**When:** Sharing client work, demonstrating expertise, problem-solution content
**Example:** How I tackled slow code reviews to ship features 3x faster.

### client-what-worked

**Formula:** What worked for [client type] (and why).
**When:** Case studies, sharing lessons from client engagements
**Example:** What worked for early-stage startups (and why).

### client-simplified

**Formula:** How I simplified [complexity] for a client.
**When:** Positioning expertise, showing value delivery
**Example:** How I simplified their 47-step deployment to 3 commands.

### client-step-by-step

**Formula:** My step-by-step for solving [problem].
**When:** Educational content, framework sharing, how-to posts
**Example:** My step-by-step for solving technical debt without stopping features.

### client-process-fix

**Formula:** The process I used to fix [X] for [ICP].
**When:** Detailed case studies, process-focused content
**Example:** The process I used to fix hiring bottlenecks for a Series A startup.

### client-thinking-behind

**Formula:** The thinking behind [key decision].
**When:** Thought leadership, strategic decisions, explaining rationale
**Example:** The thinking behind choosing monolith over microservices.

### client-what-changed

**Formula:** What changed after we fixed [problem].
**When:** Before/after content, results-focused posts
**Example:** What changed after we fixed their deployment pipeline.

### client-testimonial

**Formula:** What [client] said after working with me.
**When:** Social proof, testimonial-based content
**Example:** What a Series A founder said after working with me for 6 months.

### client-this-is-what-i-do

**Formula:** This is what I do for [specific client type].
**When:** Offer clarity, positioning, direct selling
**Example:** This is what I do for non-technical founders with a dev team.

### client-unexpected-result

**Formula:** A result I didn't expect from [project].
**When:** Authentic storytelling, surprising outcomes
**Example:** A result I didn't expect from rebuilding their auth system.

### client-transformation

**Formula:** I helped [ICP] go from [pain] to [result].
**When:** Transformation stories, clear value demonstration
**Example:** I helped a founder go from 6-month release cycles to shipping weekly.

### client-desire-process

**Formula:** How I [ICP desire] by doing [process].
**When:** Outcome-focused, process revelation
**Example:** How I reduced their AWS bill 60% by doing a 2-day audit.

### client-result-after

**Formula:** My client saw [result] after [solution].
**When:** Direct results content, proof-based posts
**Example:** My client saw 3x deployment speed after implementing CI/CD.

## Post Content Patterns

These patterns help structure the body of posts. Mix with hooks above.

### pattern-icp-pain

**Structure:**
1. Highlight a common frustration your ICP faces
2. Agitate the pain (why it's costly/urgent)
3. Show what happens if they don't fix it

**When:** Pain-focused content, problem awareness

### pattern-desires-goals

**Structure:**
1. Describe the ideal situation your ICP wants
2. Contrast their current state with the desired future
3. Show how your solution bridges the gap

**When:** Aspirational content, vision-casting

### pattern-offer-breakdown

**Structure:**
1. What your product/service is (in plain words)
2. Who it's for and who it's not for
3. What results it delivers

**When:** Direct offer posts, clarity content

### pattern-client-story

**Structure:**
1. Specific success story tied to your offer
2. Before/after transformation
3. What they did differently with your help

**When:** Case study posts, social proof

### pattern-testimonial-lesson

**Structure:**
1. Quote from a client reframed into advice
2. Story behind a testimonial
3. "This is what clients tell me most often"

**When:** Testimonial-based content, social proof with education

### pattern-objection-handling

**Structure:**
1. "I thought this wouldn't work for me until..."
2. Break down a common objection with evidence
3. Reframe doubt into confidence

**When:** Overcoming resistance, trust-building

### pattern-callout

**Structure:**
1. Speak directly to your ICP by name/role
2. "If you're a [ICP], this is for you"
3. Create exclusivity by narrowing the audience

**When:** Direct targeting, niche focus

### pattern-mini-case-study

**Structure:**
1. Quick post about a result you delivered
2. Problem → fix → measurable outcome
3. Short and punchy to drive proof

**When:** Quick wins, proof stacking

### pattern-clear-cta

**Structure:**
1. Invite readers to DM you for [specific outcome]
2. Share a link to your offer with context
3. Announce open spots, deadlines, or availability

**When:** Conversion-focused posts

### pattern-urgency-scarcity

**Structure:**
1. "X spots left / closing on [date]"
2. "This is the last time I'm offering this at [price]"
3. "Next round starts on [date]"

**When:** Launch posts, enrollment periods, limited offers

## Sales Post Workflow

How to create posts that sell:

1. **Collect ICP data:**
   - Interview former/current/future clients
   - Pull pains/desires from meeting transcripts
   - Host free workshops to collect ICP data
   - Share lead magnets in exchange for data
   - Offer free audits in exchange for data

2. **Create post:**
   - Gather all input in 1 doc
   - Use AI to help structure
   - Pick hook from library
   - Apply content pattern
   - Finish/improve yourself

3. **Mix and blend:**
   - Combine hooks with content patterns
   - Lead with pain or desire
   - Include proof (results, testimonials)
   - End with clear CTA
