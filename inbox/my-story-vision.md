# My Story & Vision

## My Story

I started as a software engineer in 2010 and quickly learned the hard truths of "work about work" in outsourcing. Instead of waiting for permission, I led client conversations, solved problems directly, and saw how fragile projects became when ownership was missing.

In 2018, I founded INNOMIZE with one goal: build with accountability, not hourly excuses. Today, we help founders and companies launch real products fast, acting as the technical owner (CTO/VP Eng) when needed, and embedding the foundations from day one so teams can focus 90% on business logic, not scaffolding.

What this means for you: if you're a founder with an idea, or a company stuck between vision and execution, I can help you go from zero to live users with speed, compliance, and focus. No vaporware, no endless delays, just outcomes.

# Quotes, Sayings & Idioms

## Tech vs Business - MVP

Stop asking: *"Is this the best structure?"*

Start asking: *"Does this get us closer to market dominance faster?"*

"Ugly but working" > "Perfect but invisible."

Architecture is not about making everything perfect. It's about knowing **when to design for the future** and **when to ship for the present**.

## Core Principles

- **Ship early**: Simplify and ship smaller.
- **Build with users**: Listen carefully and understand their problems.
- **Know what good looks like**: Find and create good things. Point out what needs to change to make it better.
- **Think in principles**: Not in playbooks.
- **Build things that last**: Put care into the foundation. Don't make it a future person's problem.
- **Avoid side quests**: Don't fix every problem. Avoid adding unnecessary process or documents.
- **Say it as it is**: Get to the point and assume good intentions.
- **Keep the team small, do more with less**: We build better with fewer people. Wear multiple hats.
- **Hire the best people**: The best people hire the best people. Help recruit.
- **Create fans**: Build a community of people who use, love, and defend the product.
